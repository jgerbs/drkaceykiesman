Details

Treatment Details

Consultation

Includes a full facial assessment and a personalized treatment plan before anything is done.

Comfort

Numbing options are available. Most treatments are well tolerated with minimal discomfort.

Downtime

Minimal. Mild swelling or bruising may occur and typically resolves within a few days.

Results Timeline

Botox peaks at around 2 weeks. Fillers are immediate and refine over the following 1 – 2 weeks.

Longevity

Botox lasts approximately 3 – 4 months. Fillers last 6 – 18 months depending on the area treated.

Aftercare

Simple post-care guidance is provided at each appointment to help you get the best results.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long do results last?

Botox lasts about 3 – 4 months. Fillers last 6 – 18 months depending on the treatment area and product used.

Is there downtime?

Minimal. You may experience mild swelling or bruising, which typically resolves within a few days. Most people return to normal activities the same day.

Will I look natural?

Yes — I take a conservative approach specifically to enhance your features, not change them. My goal is that you look refreshed, not done.

Do I need a consultation first?

Yes. A consultation ensures treatment is safe, appropriate, and fully tailored to your goals. Treatment may be done the same day depending on your needs.

How do I know how many units I'll need?

This is assessed during your consultation based on your facial anatomy and desired outcome. I'll give you a clear estimate before any treatment begins.
