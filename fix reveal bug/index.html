About Dr. Kiesman

Hello, I'm Dr. Kacey Kiesman

I'm a licensed Naturopathic Doctor and Registered Nurse practicing in Kelowna, BC. My practice is built on the belief that lasting health comes from understanding the whole person instead of just managing symptoms.

I combine evidence-based naturopathic medicine with modern diagnostics to create individualized care plans that address root causes and support your body's natural ability to heal. Whether you're navigating hormonal shifts, gut issues, fatigue, or simply want to feel your best, I'm here to help you get there.

My Story

From Prince Rupert
to Root Cause Medicine

Growing up in Prince Rupert, BC gave me a deep appreciation for community, resilience, and the power of nature. When I moved to Vancouver to pursue my nursing degree, I discovered a passion for understanding why people get sick — not just how to treat them once they are.

That curiosity led me to naturopathic medicine and, eventually, to the world of natural aesthetics. I believe that how you feel and how you look are deeply connected — and that both deserve thoughtful, personalized care.
