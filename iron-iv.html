What to Expect

About Iron Infusions

Results

  • Improved energy levels and reduced fatigue
  • Correction of iron deficiency more efficiently than oral supplements
  • May support cognitive function and exercise tolerance
  • Full iron store replenishment typically over 4–8 weeks

Ideal For

  • Iron deficiency or iron-deficiency anemia
  • Those who cannot tolerate or absorb oral iron
  • Post-surgical or postpartum iron depletion
  • Chronic fatigue linked to low iron levels

How It Works

Iron is delivered directly into the bloodstream through an IV, bypassing the digestive tract for full absorption. The infusion runs slowly over approximately 90 minutes while you are comfortably monitored. A consult and bloodwork review are required before your first infusion to confirm suitability and determine the appropriate iron preparation.

Details

Treatment Details

Consult First

A pre-iron IV consult is required before your infusion. Bring any existing bloodwork or have it sent in advance. Additional labs may be ordered if needed.

Iron Ordering

Once your consult is complete and iron has been prescribed, it will be ordered and shipped to the clinic. You will be notified when it has arrived and your infusion appointment can be booked.

Duration

The infusion appointment is 105 minutes. Iron must be administered slowly to minimize the risk of adverse reactions, and you are monitored throughout.

Downtime

Most patients can resume normal activities the same day. Some may experience mild fatigue or a headache after the infusion, which typically resolves quickly.

Safety Monitoring

You will be monitored during and after the infusion for any signs of reaction. Your health history is reviewed at the consult to ensure the treatment is safe and appropriate for you.

Before Your Visit

Eat a light meal and stay well hydrated before your infusion appointment. Avoid arriving on an empty stomach.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need bloodwork before iron IV therapy?

Yes. A pre-iron IV consult is required before treatment. If you have existing bloodwork, please send it in prior to the visit. If additional labs are needed to confirm suitability, they will be ordered at that appointment.

How long does an iron infusion take?

The iron IV therapy appointment is 105 minutes. This allows time for the infusion to be administered slowly and safely, and for monitoring during and after treatment.

How soon will I feel better after an iron infusion?

Many patients notice improvements in energy and fatigue within 1–2 weeks, with full iron stores typically replenishing over 4–8 weeks. Individual results vary depending on the severity of deficiency.

Who is iron IV therapy for?

Iron IV therapy is suitable for people with confirmed iron deficiency or iron-deficiency anemia who have not responded adequately to oral iron supplements, or who cannot tolerate them. Suitability is confirmed at your pre-iron consult.

Are there any side effects?

Most people tolerate iron infusions well. Mild side effects may include temporary nausea, headache, or discomfort at the IV site. You will be monitored throughout the infusion. Serious reactions are rare but possible, and your provider will review your history before proceeding.

Is iron IV therapy covered by MSP or insurance?

Iron IV therapy is a private service and is not covered by MSP. Some extended health plans may provide partial coverage. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies.
