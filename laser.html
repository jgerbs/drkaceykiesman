What to Expect

About the Treatment

Results

  • Long-lasting hair reduction
  • Smoother skin with reduced shaving frequency
  • Targets multiple areas from face to full body
  • Comfort-forward treatment with integrated cooling

Ideal For

  • Unwanted facial or body hair
  • Ingrown hairs or irritation from shaving
  • Clients wanting lower-maintenance grooming
  • Those ready for a consistent treatment plan

How It Works

DiolazeXL uses targeted light energy to safely and effectively reduce unwanted hair. Laser pulses are absorbed by the pigment in the hair, traveling down the shaft to heat and disable the follicle. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple sessions are needed to target all follicles during their active growth phase. Built-in cooling technology enhances comfort throughout treatment.

Details

Treatment Details

Time

Appointments range from 30–90 minutes depending on the treatment area and whether you are booking a single area or a bundled service.

Downtime

Minimal. Mild redness or sensitivity may occur for a few hours after treatment. Most people return to normal activities the same day.

Customization

Treatment is tailored to your skin type, hair type, and the area being treated. Your provider will help select the most appropriate plan based on your goals.

Safety Screening

A consultation is recommended before treatment to review your history, skin and hair type, recent sun exposure, medications, and any factors that may affect safety or results.

Best Results

Laser hair removal works best as a series because hair grows in cycles. Most patients need 6–10 sessions spaced several weeks apart for the most noticeable long-term reduction.

Aftercare

Arrive with the area shaved. Avoid waxing or plucking between sessions, limit sun exposure, and follow your provider's aftercare guidance to support the best results.

FAQ

Common Questions

How many sessions will I need?

Most patients need a series of treatments for best results. A typical plan is 6–10 sessions spaced several weeks apart, depending on the area treated, hair type, and hair growth cycle.

When will I see results?

Hair gradually becomes thinner and grows back more slowly after each session. Noticeable reduction usually occurs after a few treatments, with continued improvement over the full series.

Is there downtime?

Downtime is minimal. You may have mild redness or sensitivity for a few hours after treatment, but most people return to normal activities the same day.

Does it hurt?

Most patients describe the feeling as a quick snap or warmth on the skin. DiolazeXL is designed with built-in cooling to help keep treatment as comfortable as possible.

Is DiolazeXL permanent?

Laser hair removal provides long-term hair reduction. Some hair may grow back over time, but it is usually finer and lighter. Maintenance treatments may be recommended.

What areas can be treated?

DiolazeXL can be used on most areas of the body, including the face, underarms, bikini area, legs, back, chest, and arms. Your provider will help choose the best treatment plan based on your goals.

Who should avoid this treatment?

Laser hair removal may not be recommended for people with active skin infections or open wounds, certain skin conditions in the treatment area, recent sunburn or heavy tanning, or use of medications that increase light sensitivity. Your provider will review your history before treatment.

Is laser hair removal covered by MSP or insurance?

No. Laser hair removal is considered a cosmetic treatment and is not covered by MSP or insurance plans.
