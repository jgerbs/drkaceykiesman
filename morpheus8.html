What to Expect

Treatment Steps

Results

  • Skin tightening and firming
  • Improved texture and tone
  • Softening of fine lines and wrinkles over time
  • Continued collagen remodeling for progressive results
  • Facial contouring

Ideal For

  • Mild to moderate skin laxity
  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Uneven texture or enlarged pores
  • Acne scars
  • Those seeking a non-surgical option

My Approach

Morpheus8 Burst combines microneedling with radiofrequency energy to target deeper layers of the skin. The controlled heat stimulates collagen and elastin production, helping to tighten, smooth, and remodel the skin over time. Each patient is assessed individually to determine the appropriate settings and treatment plan.

Details

Treatment Details

Time

105 minutes (1 hour 45 minutes). This allows time for assessment, prep, numbing, treatment, and post-care.

Downtime

Most clients should plan for 1 to 2 days of redness, swelling, and visible skin sensitivity after treatment.

Customization

Treatment depth and settings are tailored to the area treated, your skin concerns, and the amount of tightening or contouring needed.

Safety Screening

Your provider will review your medical history, skin health, healing history, and treatment goals to make sure Morpheus8 is appropriate for you.

Best Results

Most patients benefit from a series of 3 to 4 treatments spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart, with improvement continuing as collagen rebuilds.

Aftercare

You will receive personalized aftercare instructions. Makeup is generally avoided for 48 hours, and gentle skincare plus sun protection are recommended while the skin heals.

FAQ

Common Questions

How many sessions will I need?

Most patients need a series of treatments for best results. A common plan is 3–4 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart, depending on the area treated and the amount of tightening needed.

When will I see results?

Results develop gradually as collagen rebuilds. Most patients see continued improvement over several weeks after each treatment, with final results at about 3 months post-treatment.

What does Morpheus8 help with?

Morpheus8 is commonly used to improve skin laxity, facial contouring, fine lines and wrinkles, acne scars, uneven texture, and enlarged pores. It works by combining microneedling with radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen and elastin, restoring structure and elasticity in the skin.

Is Morpheus8 the same as microneedling?

No. Morpheus8 uses deeper microneedling combined with radiofrequency energy, allowing treatment to reach deeper layers of the skin and provide more skin tightening and facial contouring compared to standard microneedling.

Can I wear makeup after treatment?

It is generally recommended to avoid makeup for 48 hours after treatment to allow the skin to heal. Personalized aftercare instructions are provided at each appointment.

Who should avoid this treatment?

Morpheus8 may not be recommended for people with active skin infections or open wounds, certain skin conditions in the treatment area, a history of poor healing or keloid scarring, or certain medical conditions or medications that affect healing. Your provider will review your history before treatment.

Is Morpheus8 covered by MSP or insurance?

No. Morpheus8 is considered a cosmetic treatment and is not covered by MSP or insurance plans.

Do I need a consultation first?

Yes. A consultation is recommended to review your skin, discuss your goals, and make sure Morpheus8 is the right treatment for you. Treatment may begin the same day depending on your needs and assessment.
