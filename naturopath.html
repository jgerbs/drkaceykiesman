My Approach

I take the time to understand your full picture — not just your current symptoms, but your history, lifestyle, and goals. Every care plan is individualized. I use evidence-based tools including functional lab testing, nutritional medicine, botanical therapy, and lifestyle counselling to support your body's natural ability to heal and thrive.

Details

Visit Details

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment is a full 60-minute session covering your complete health history, current concerns, lifestyle, and goals. We'll build your care plan together before you leave.

Follow-Up Visits

Return visits range from 15 to 45 minutes depending on what needs to be addressed. Choose the length that fits your needs — your provider can help guide this if you're unsure.

Lab Testing

Functional lab work is often recommended to get a clearer picture of what's happening beneath the surface. This may include hormonal panels, nutrient levels, thyroid function, and more.

Insurance Coverage

Many extended health plans cover naturopathic visits. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies. Contact us before your first appointment to confirm your coverage.

Prescription Fill

Existing patients can request a prescription refill for a flat fee of $30. This applies to active naturopathic prescriptions and does not require a full follow-up appointment.

Before Your Visit

Bring any recent lab results, a list of current medications and supplements, and notes on your top health concerns. The more context you can share, the more we can accomplish together.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is naturopathic medicine?

Naturopathic medicine is a regulated health profession that combines evidence-based natural therapies with modern diagnostics. Naturopathic doctors are trained to identify root causes of illness and create individualized treatment plans that may include nutrition, botanical medicine, lifestyle counselling, and lab testing.

Is naturopathic medicine covered by insurance?

Many extended health plans in BC cover naturopathic visits. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies. It's worth checking your plan before your appointment — most people are surprised by how much is covered.

What should I expect at my first visit?

Your initial consultation is 60 minutes. We'll go through your complete health history, current concerns, diet, lifestyle, and goals. By the end of the appointment, you'll have a clear care plan with specific next steps.

How long does it take to see results?

This depends on what you're working on. Some people notice changes quickly — within a few weeks. Others, particularly those dealing with hormonal or chronic digestive issues, may need 2–3 months to see meaningful progress. Your care plan will set realistic timelines.

Do you work alongside my family doctor?

Yes. Naturopathic medicine works best as a complement to your conventional medical care. I'm happy to communicate with your GP or specialist when appropriate and to review any recent lab work or investigations you've had done.

Which follow-up visit length should I book?

If you're unsure, the 30-minute follow-up is a good default for most check-ins. Book the 45-minute option if you have multiple concerns to cover or are due for a more comprehensive review. The 15- or 20-minute options work well for focused questions or lab result reviews.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed to see a naturopathic doctor. You can book directly online at any time.
