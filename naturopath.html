Visit Details

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment is a full 60-minute session covering your complete health history, current concerns, lifestyle, and goals. Labs and/or testing may be recommended to gain further insight into your current health picture. We'll build your initial care plan together before you leave.

Follow-Up Visits

Return visits range from 15 to 45 minutes depending on what needs to be addressed. Choose the length that realistically fits your needs, and if you're unsure your provider can help guide this.

Lab Testing

Functional lab testing is often recommended to get a clearer picture of what's happening beneath the surface. This may include hormonal panels, thyroid function, nutrient levels, GI stool testing, and more.

Insurance Coverage

Many extended health plans cover naturopathic visits. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies. It is recommended that you reach out to your insurance provider prior to your first visit to learn about your naturopathic coverage.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is naturopathic medicine?

Naturopathic medicine is a regulated health profession that combines evidence-based natural therapies with modern diagnostics, prescription medications when appropriate, and a strong focus on prevention. Care is centered on identifying and addressing the root causes of illness or imbalance through individualized treatment plans that may include nutrition, supplements, lifestyle support, diagnostic testing, and prescriptions when needed. The goal is to support the whole person and promote long-term, sustainable health.

Is naturopathic medicine covered by insurance?

Many extended health plans in BC cover naturopathic visits. We offer direct billing to most insurance companies. I recommend checking your plan before your appointment to see what coverage you have.

What should I expect at my first visit?

Your initial consultation is 60 minutes. We'll go through your complete health history, current concerns, diet, lifestyle, and goals. By the end of the appointment, you'll have an initial care plan with specific next steps.

How long does it take to see results?

This depends on what you're working on. Some people notice changes quickly within a few weeks. Others, particularly those dealing with hormonal or chronic digestive issues, may need a few months to see meaningful progress. We will assess and set realistic timelines at your initial visit.

Do you work alongside my family doctor?

Yes. Naturopathic medicine works best as a complement to your conventional medical care. I'm happy to communicate with your GP or specialist when appropriate and to review any recent lab work or investigations you've had done.

Which follow-up visit length should I book?

If you're unsure, the 30-minute follow-up is a good default for most check-ins. Book the 45-minute option if you have multiple concerns to cover or are due for a more comprehensive review. The 15- or 20-minute options work well for focused questions or lab result reviews.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed to see a naturopathic doctor. You can book directly online at any time.
